FAQ

Questions Palworld players usually ask.

Here are answers to the most common hosting questions. We’ll keep this updated as the game — and SideQuest Servers — evolve.

Where are your servers located?

We’re a US‑based team and start with US regions first. As our community grows, we’ll add more locations based on where people are actually playing.

Can I change my player count later?

Yes. You can move up to a higher slot plan as your group grows. We try to keep upgrades simple, honest, and clear.

Do I really have full control?

Yes. You get access to configs, files, and scheduled tasks through the panel, so you can tune your Palworld world to your group instead of being stuck with defaults.

What about backups and restarts?

Every plan includes automatic daily backups and the ability to schedule restarts. We treat your world as something worth protecting, not just a save file.
